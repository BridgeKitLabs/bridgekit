# **BridgeKit**

**The Stripe for BNB Chain — Web2 SaaS & Marketplace Payments Made Easy**

BridgeKit is a **developer-first SDK** that makes it simple for Web2 developers to integrate **BNB Chain payments, wallets, and blockchain functionality** into their apps and marketplaces.

Our mission is to **remove friction, abstract blockchain complexity**, and let developers focus on building great apps while BridgeKit handles the BNB integration.

---

## **Key Features (Planned)**

* **Wallet Integration** – Seamlessly connect user wallets (MetaMask, WalletConnect).
* **Payments** – Accept BNB and other tokens, with automated transaction verification.
* **Modular Adapters** – Future-proof architecture for additional integrations (AI billing, NFTs, subscriptions, analytics).
* **Developer-Friendly APIs** – Simple endpoints, React components, and SDK methods.
* **Sandbox & Testnet Support** – Easily test integrations before going live.

---

## **Concept / Vision**

BridgeKit enables **Web2 developers and SaaS teams** to integrate blockchain payments as easily as they do with Stripe. The SDK provides:

1. **Core Payment Adapters:** Plug-and-play modules for authentication, subscriptions, usage-based billing, and marketplaces.
2. **Future Modules / Adapters:** Expand functionality for AI-driven billing, NFTs, workflow-triggered payments, and analytics dashboards.
3. **Developer Sandbox & Documentation:** Simple onboarding, guides, and working code examples to reduce integration friction.

Our goal is to **make BNB Chain the default payment layer** for modern web apps and marketplaces without requiring developers to become blockchain experts.

---

## **Planned Roadmap**

**Phase 1 – MVP / Core SDK**

* Wallet integration, basic payment requests, and usage-based billing.
* Sandbox environment for testing and documentation.

**Phase 2 – Platform Integrations**

* Integrate with SaaS and web platforms like AISDK, WooCommerce, Clerk, and betterAuth.
* Add transaction monitoring, webhooks, and dashboard metrics.

**Phase 3 – Mainnet Release & Expansion**

* Continuous testing, bug fixes, and stability improvements.
* Full documentation, developer guides, and release of additional adapters.

---

## **Why BridgeKit is Innovative**

* **Web2 Developer-Focused:** Unlike most BNB Chain tools targeting crypto-native devs, BridgeKit is designed for SaaS and web app teams.
* **Modular & Extensible:** Add new adapters over time for new use cases.
* **Plug-and-Play:** Developers can integrate payments and blockchain features without rewriting workflows.
* **Scalable:** Designed for marketplaces, SaaS platforms, and AI tools.
